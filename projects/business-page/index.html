Our Story: More Than Just Coffee

What started as a way to make someone's day turned into a full coffee experience. It began with a simple game of guessing the color inside my coffee mug. On days I guessed right, the coffee somehow felt even better.

Now, our shop brings this fun to life — creating connection and excitement with every cup.

Our Mission

To deliver an experience that makes people’s day.

Through playfulness, hospitality, and consistent quality, we turn everyday routines into moments worth smiling about. Whether it’s the Color Guessing Game, a quick laugh, or a selfie with our famous Banana Bread Latte — we’re here to make your day.
