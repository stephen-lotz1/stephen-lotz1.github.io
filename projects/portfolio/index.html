Stephen Lotz

I’m a freelance journalist covering local government and community stories in Greenbelt, Maryland. My reporting for the Greenbelt News Review explores how policy decisions affect residents’ lives, and my multimedia projects at the University of Maryland focus on storytelling, design, and audience engagement.

Through both print and digital work, I aim to make civic issues more accessible to the communities they affect.
